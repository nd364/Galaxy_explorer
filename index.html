Supernovas Info

A supernova is a powerful and luminous explosion of a star. A supernova occurs during the last evolutionary stages of a massive star.

Supernovae can briefly outshine entire galaxies.

Black Hole Info

A black hole is a region of space where gravity is so strong that nothing, not even light, can escape.

Supermassive black holes are located at the centres of galaxies.
